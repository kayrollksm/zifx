How ZIFX Works

ZIFX is not a fixed-return investment program. Your capital is traded using structured, risk-controlled strategies in the forex and cryptocurrency markets.

At the end of each trading cycle, we look at the actual account balance. If there is profit, it is shared. If there is no profit, no fee is charged.

ZIFX does not earn from deposits, subscriptions, or hidden charges. Our incentive is fully aligned with trader performance.

Our Core Principle

ZIFX earns only when traders earn. If trading performance is negative, no performance fee is charged. This ensures transparency, fairness, and long-term sustainability.
